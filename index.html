Our home is dying. It is our responsibility to save it.
The pollution humans create destroys Earth's protective shields from the inside. As long as we continue to create pollution at such a massive rate, the Earth's Ozone Layer will continue to weaken. A weak Ozone Layer results in more of the energy from the sun penetrating our atmosphere.

We experience the changes this has on our environment everyday, as tempurture around the globe steadily rises. However, the biggest effects of a weak Ozone Layer are not as obvious. Earth's waters have absorbed more than 90% of the energy trapped on Earth by greenhouse gasses since 1955.

Ocean Warming is the main cause of massive coral bleaching around the world, ice caps melting, and sea levels rising.
What the heck is Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation?

The AMOC is a natural system of ocean currents that carries warm water North from the equator. The process ensures that Earth's ocean waters consistently distribute heat and energy from the sun across the hemispheres.

Scientists have been monitering the AMOC since 2004. Future projections suggest that as climate change heats up Earth's water systems, the outcome of the AMOC will become more drastic.
